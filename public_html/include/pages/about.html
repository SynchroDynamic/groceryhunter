Points and Levels

Levels

Levels are based on points:

Level 1

With a white Badge a user can add products and additional prices.

Level 2

With a yellow badge a user can mark reviews as helpful

An orange badge also allows you to mark a price as reliable

Level 3

With an orange badge a user can mark reviews as unhelpful

An orange badge also allows you to mark a price as unreliable

Level 4

With a green badge a user can mark products for deletion

Level 5

With a blue badge a user can write reviews

Level 6

With a purple badge a user can add a new product Category

Level 7

With a red badge a user has access to edit other user's products.

Level 8

With a black badge a user has full access to a product. They have the additional privilege of marking a user as unruly

Points

A Contributor receives points for the following action:

Product Add

Adding a product attributes 10 Points to your Total

Product Edit

Editing a product attributes 2 points to your Total

Product Review

Reviewing a product attributes 10 points to your Total

Flags

There are two Types of Flags at Grocery Hunter

Green Flags

You receive an additional point for a green flag.

Red Flags

You will lose 2 points if you are given a red flag.
